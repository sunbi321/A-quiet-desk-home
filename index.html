Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 3, 2026

A quiet desk (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how you can manage your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use the app:

Account Information
If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive basic account information such as your user ID and, depending on the provider, your email address or account identifier. This is used to create and manage your app account.

User Content
The app allows you to create spaces, folders, and memos. If you are not signed in, this data is stored locally on your device. If you sign in, your spaces and memos may be synced to our backend service so you can access them with your account.

AI Camera Data
When you use the AI Camera feature, you may take a photo or select an image from your photo library. The image is sent to our backend service and processed by an AI service to detect items or generate recipe recommendations. We use the image only to provide the requested AI feature. The app saves the resulting item names or memo content only when they are added to your spaces.

Purchase and Credit Information
If you purchase AI Camera credits or receive credits through rewarded ads, we store information needed to manage your AI Camera credit balance, such as free credits, paid credits, rewarded-ad cooldown time, and processed transaction identifiers. We do not collect or store your payment card information.

Advertising Data
The app may show banner ads and rewarded ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, ad interaction data, and related information according to Google’s privacy policies.

Device and Usage Information
The app and its service providers may process technical information necessary to operate the app, such as authentication tokens, app settings, network requests, and error information.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the app
  • Save and sync your spaces, folders, and memos
  • Authenticate users
  • Provide AI Camera analysis and recipe recommendations
  • Manage AI Camera credits and in-app purchases
  • Show ads and rewarded ads
  • Improve app reliability and security
  • Respond to user requests and support issues
  • Delete accounts and related data when requested

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services to provide its features, including:

  • Supabase for authentication, database storage, backend functions, and account management
  • Apple Sign in with Apple for authentication
  • Google Sign-In for authentication
  • Google Gemini for AI image analysis and recipe recommendation features
  • Google AdMob for banner ads and rewarded ads
  • Apple App Store for in-app purchase processing

These third-party services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

4. Photos and Camera Access

The app requests camera and photo library access only when you choose to use the AI Camera feature. We do not access your camera or photo library unless you select this feature. Images are used to analyze visible items and provide the requested result.

5. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store your credit card details. We only store information necessary to confirm and apply AI Camera credits to your account.

6. Data Storage and Retention

Local memo data may remain on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.

If you sign in, synced data may be stored on our backend service. We keep your account data, memo data, and AI Camera credit information for as long as needed to provide the app, unless you delete your account or request deletion.

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account from within the app. After signing in, open the menu on the Home screen and select “Delete Account.” Deleting your account removes your account, synced spaces, memos, and AI Camera usage data from our backend.

8. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only with service providers that help us operate the app, process authentication, provide AI features, process purchases, show ads, or comply with legal obligations.

9. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under the age required by applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. International Data Processing

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your country of residence. By using the app, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed by third-party service providers in other regions.

12. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or request information about your personal data. You may also delete your account directly in the app.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the effective date above.

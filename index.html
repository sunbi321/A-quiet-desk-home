Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 3, 2026

A quiet desk (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how you can manage your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information
If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive basic account information such as your user ID and, depending on the provider, your email address or account identifier.

User Content
The app allows you to create spaces, folders, and memos. If you are not signed in, this data is stored locally on your device. If you sign in, your spaces and memos may be synced to our backend service.

AI Camera Data
When you use the AI Camera feature, you may take a photo or select an image from your photo library. The image is sent to our backend service and processed by AI services to detect items or generate recipe recommendations.

Purchase and Credit Information
If you purchase AI Camera credits or receive credits through rewarded ads, we store information necessary to manage your AI Camera credit balance.

Advertising Data
The app may show banner ads and rewarded ads through Google AdMob.

Device and Usage Information
The app and its service providers may process technical information necessary to operate the app.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate the app
  • Save and sync your spaces, folders, and memos
  • Authenticate users
  • Provide AI Camera analysis and recipe recommendations
  • Manage AI Camera credits and in-app purchases
  • Show ads and rewarded ads
  • Improve app reliability and security
  • Respond to support requests
  • Delete accounts and related data when requested

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services including:

  • Supabase
  • Apple Sign in with Apple
  • Google Sign-In
  • Google Gemini
  • Google AdMob
  • Apple App Store

4. Photos and Camera Access

The app requests camera and photo library access only when you choose to use the AI Camera feature.

5. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store your credit card details.

6. Data Storage and Retention

Local memo data may remain on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.

If you sign in, synced data may be stored on our backend service until account deletion is requested.

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account from within the app. After signing in, open the menu on the Home screen and select “Delete Account.”

8. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Information may only be shared with service providers required to operate the app or comply with legal obligations.

9. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under the age required by applicable law.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

11. International Data Processing

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your country of residence.

12. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or request information about your personal data.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If significant changes are made, the effective date will be updated.

14. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us at:

info@anshin-labs.com
